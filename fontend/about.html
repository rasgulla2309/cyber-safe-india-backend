About Cyber Safe India

Empowering users with cybersecurity awareness

Our Mission

Cyber Safe India is an independent cybersecurity awareness platform created to help individuals recognize digital fraud risks and make safer online decisions. The platform focuses on community-driven protection, education, and transparency.

Platform Capabilities

Phone Intelligence

Identify suspicious or reported numbers through community data.

Link Safety Checking

Evaluate URLs to reduce phishing exposure risk.

Fraud Reporting

Contribute warnings that protect other users.

Threat Awareness

Stay informed about current cyber fraud patterns.

Project Background

This system was developed as part of hands-on cybersecurity learning and research into defensive digital tools. It demonstrates how community data and simple intelligence mechanisms can improve public safety awareness.

Transparency Notice

This platform is NOT affiliated with any government agency, law enforcement organization, or official cybersecurity authority. All results are informational and community-driven.
